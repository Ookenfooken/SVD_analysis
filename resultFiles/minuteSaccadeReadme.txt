1. column - patient:
0 --> control
1 --> patient

2. column - subjectID: 
This doesn't correspond to the full subject ID. We are reading out the last 2 digits of the subject ID

3. column:
number of large target-directed saccades

4. column:
mean accuracy of large target-directed saccades

5. column:
number of small corrective saccades

6. column:
mean accuracy of small corrective saccades

7. column:
amplitude of corrective saccades
--> negative amplitude means initial overshoot and vice versa

8. column:
number of blinks during minute of saccading
